About me

I graduated in 2023 from the University of Arkansas with a B.S. in Geology and a minor in Chemistry. I am currently working at a Geophysical engineering startup in Siloam Springs, AR. My primary interests include using remote sensing data to study the Earth and Solar System.

In recent years, scientists have become capable of collecting unprecedented amounts of data from Earth and space environments using rapidly advancing hardware. I am keen to discover novel ways to leverage data analysis, computational modeling, and machine learning techniques to solve important problems in processing this data, making it available to the public, and learning more about our Universe.
